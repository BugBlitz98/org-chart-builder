Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 03, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Org Chart Builder is designed to prioritize your privacy. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous data about how the app is used (e.g., session duration, feature usage) to improve performance.
  • Account Information: If you choose to sign in, we store your name and email to sync your charts across devices.
  • Purchase Data: We track subscription status and purchase tokens to ensure your premium benefits are active.

2. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Our application offers premium features via in-app purchases and subscriptions:

  • Transactions are processed securely through the Google Play Store.
  • We do not store your credit card or payment information on our servers.
  • Subscription status is verified using secure receipts provided by the app store.

3. Data Storage & Retention

Your charts are stored securely to ensure you can access them when needed. Our storage policy depends on your subscription status:

Important for Free Users: Data for free accounts is stored for 7 days. After 7 days of inactivity, charts are automatically deleted from our cloud servers.
  • Free Users: Charts are kept for 7 days after your last activity. Accessing or saving a chart resets this timer.
  • Premium Users: Subscribers enjoy permanent cloud storage. Your data will not be subject to automatic deletion while your subscription is active.
  • Local Storage: A copy of your data may be stored locally on your device for offline access.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers for specific functionalities:

  • Google Play Services (Payments & Ad Management)
  • Convex (Cloud Database & Backend)
  • Analytics providers (Anonymous performance tracking)

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your data at any time. You can choose to use the app anonymously as a guest, which will keep your data stored only on your physical device and subject to our 7-day retention policy if synced to the cloud.
